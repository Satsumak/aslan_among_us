OVERVIEW

Among Us is an online sci-fi murder-mystery game by Innersloth, and was released on June 15, 2018, on iOS, Android, and Steam on August 18, 2018. The game is set in a spaceship, which contains a group of crew members where there is, among them, an impostor who wants to sabotage the ship and kill everyone, where they must do the tasks and find out who the impostor is.

Logo of Among Us developer, Innersloth

Among Us is set in The Skeld, which is the main map of the game set in a spaceship. Starting in 2019, two new maps were launched: Mira HQ, which is set on a space base, on August 8, 2019, and Polus, which is set on a planet, on November 12, 2019. Starting in 2020, was considered by Innersloth a complete game, and as of June 11, 2020, the two new maps, which were previously paid for, were released, both for mobile and PC, for free for players to host. Among Us 2, according to the company, was scheduled to be available by 2021, but is now cancelled and work will proceed on improving the original game instead. In addition, many merchandises, such as T-shirts, toys, have been announced and launched.

Original artwork from the game

The game takes place on a space ship in outer space. There are two teams: the Impostors and the Crewmates. There can be a total of 10 players in an online game, with up to three impostors. The Impostors' objective is to eliminate all Crewmates using either aggressive, defensive, or passive tactics before the Crewmates complete all of their tasks or discover their true identity. The Crewmates' objective is to complete all of their tasks while figuring out who the Impostor is.
